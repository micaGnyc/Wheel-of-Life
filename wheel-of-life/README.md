# Wheel of Life - Future Coach Prototype

A personalized life satisfaction assessment tool and Wheel of Life coaching exercise with AI-generated coaching reports, built for Future Coach.

**Live Demo:** https://wheel-of-life-tau.vercel.app/

## About

The Wheel of Life is a coaching tool that helps young people (ages 16-24) see how satisfied they are across different areas of their life—and where they might want to focus next. Built on the research-validated Brief Multidimensional Students' Life Satisfaction Scale (BMSLSS).

## Features

- **Life Satisfaction Assessment** - Rate satisfaction across 6 areas (Family, Friends, School/Work, Living Space, Self, Overall)
- **Custom Life Areas** - Add your own areas to track (e.g., Health, Finances, Hobbies)
- **Visual Wheel** - Animated radar chart showing life balance at a glance
- **Prioritization Questions** - Identify what matters most and where to focus
- **AI-Powered Report** - Personalized coaching insights generated by Claude AI

## User Flow

1. **Welcome** - Read intro and framing
2. **Demographics** - Enter age, gender, ethnicity
3. **Assessment** - Rate satisfaction in each life area (Terrible → Delighted)
4. **Custom Area** - Optionally add a personal life area
5. **View Wheel** - See visualization of life balance
6. **Prioritize** - Select what matters most and what to work on
7. **Deep Dive** - Explain why and what "better" looks like
8. **AI Report** - Receive personalized coaching insights

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, React, Tailwind CSS
- **AI:** Claude Sonnet 4.5 (Anthropic API)
- **UI Components:** Shadcn/ui
- **Deployment:** Vercel
